Solar Energy Optimization Through IoT Analytics

As solar becomes mainstream, efficiency is paramount. IoT analytics can unlock hidden potential in solar PV systems by offering real-time insights.

IoT for Smarter Solar

IoT devices monitor sunlight exposure, panel temperature, inverter efficiency, and environmental factors like dust or bird droppings. With this data, operators can optimize panel alignment, initiate cleaning, and predict output.

Benefits

  • Improved ROI through higher uptime
  • Detection of underperforming panels
  • Forecasting generation against consumption trends
