About EddyBits Innovations

Pioneering IoT and Power Electronics Solutions for Industrial Excellence

Founded in 2019, EddyBits Innovations has emerged as India's trusted partner in digital transformation, specializing in intelligent IoT connectivity and cutting-edge power electronics solutions that drive efficiency, sustainability, and innovation across industries.

Our Story

EddyBits Innovations Private Limited was founded with a vision to revolutionize industrial operations through intelligent technology solutions. Since our inception in 2019, we have been at the forefront of India's industrial digital transformation, serving clients across diverse sectors with innovative IoT and power electronics solutions.

Our expertise spans industrial IoT systems, custom power electronics, BLDC controllers, and EV solutions, enabling businesses to achieve unprecedented levels of efficiency, reliability, and sustainability in their operations.
