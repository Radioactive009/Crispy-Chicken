Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 10th June, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how EddyBits Innovations Private Limited ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website www.eddybits.com and any associated services, platforms, or applications.

By continuing to browse or use our website, you agree that we can store and access cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) by websites you visit. They help make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the site owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

a. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for you to browse the website and use its features (e.g., login sessions, secure areas). Disabling them may affect site performance.

b. Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website (e.g., which pages are visited most often). We use tools like Google Analytics to analyze site traffic and performance. All data is anonymized.

c. Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you’ve made (such as language preferences) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.

d. Third-Party Cookies

We may use cookies from third-party services (e.g., social media integrations, embedded videos) to enhance user experience. These cookies are subject to the third-party providers’ privacy policies.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • Ensure the website functions correctly
  • Understand user interactions and site traffic
  • Improve user experience and product features
  • Provide secure login and form submission processes

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Note: Disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features on our site.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically for updates.

6. Contact Us
